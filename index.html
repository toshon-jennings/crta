About CRTA

I founded CRTA to expand access to tech careers for middle and high school students, as well as adult learners. Our model is certification-first pathways designed to help students graduate not only with diplomas, but with industry-recognized credentials. From CompTIA to Microsoft tracks, the goal is equity, readiness, and career acceleration. It's where mentorship meets methodology, tailored to your school's culture.

Our goal: plant the seeds of tomorrow's tech field today.
